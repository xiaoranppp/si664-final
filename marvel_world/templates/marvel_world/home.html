Hi! Welcome to the marvel hero website designed by Xianran. This app is a class homework of SI664 in the university of Michigan

On the Character page, you can click the name of the Super hero to see all the information about this hero

On the Chracter Filter page, you can search for heros by their name, super power, gender, alignment, race, publisher, skin color, eye color, hair color weight and height

On the Comic page, you can explore the information of each comic by click their name.

On the Comic Filter page, you can search a comic by its name, year when it was produced, description and characters in these comics.

On the Super Power page, you can get the characters who have aspecific super power by click the name of the super power.
